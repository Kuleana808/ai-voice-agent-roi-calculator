Auto repair diagnostic update script library

Use these as practical starting points, not compliance or customer-service policy advice. The job is simple: explain what the technician found, keep the customer moving toward a decision, and preserve trust when diagnostics, extra teardown, or estimate questions would otherwise stall the job.

Inspection received

Acknowledge the vehicle is in diagnosis and promise the next update window

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. We have your [Vehicle] checked in and our technician is working through the diagnostic process now.

I wanted to confirm we have it in progress and should have the next update for you around [Time / Day]. If anything urgent shows up before then, we will call or text you right away at this number.
  • Best for calming anxious customers right after drop-off or tow-in.
  • Sets a clear next-update expectation so the customer does not call repeatedly for status.
Findings ready

Explain that the shop has diagnostic findings and is ready to review the next step

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] at [Shop Name]. We finished the diagnostic review on your [Vehicle] and we have the findings ready to go over with you.

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] when you have a minute so we can walk through the issue, the recommended repair, and the next-step timing.
  • Useful when the real bottleneck is getting the customer to engage with the results.
  • Frames the callback around clarity and next steps, not pressure.
Additional teardown

Ask for approval when the technician needs more teardown or testing to confirm the root cause

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. Our technician has narrowed down the issue on your [Vehicle], but we need [additional teardown / more testing / extra diagnostic time] to confirm the root cause before we recommend the right repair.

If you approve that next step, call or text us back at [Phone Number] and we will keep the diagnosis moving instead of losing more time.
  • Best for jobs that cannot be priced confidently without a bit more labor or disassembly.
  • Makes the reason for the extra step concrete instead of sounding like vague upsell language.
No-answer callback

Recover the diagnostic handoff when the customer misses the first call

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] at [Shop Name]. I just tried to reach you with an update on your [Vehicle]. We have the diagnostic findings ready and need to review the next step with you.

Call or text us back at [Phone Number] when you can. The faster we connect, the faster we can keep the repair moving.
  • Useful after a voicemail or missed live call when the advisor needs a short, repeatable follow-up.
  • Creates urgency without sounding harsh.
Estimate ready

Bridge from diagnosis into estimate approval cleanly

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. We have completed the diagnostic work on your [Vehicle] and the estimate for the recommended repair is ready.

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] so we can review the repair plan, total, and timing. Once you approve it, we can move the job forward.
  • Best for moving the customer from “what is wrong?” into “do I authorize it?”
  • Pairs well with estimate reminder and approval follow-up workflows if the first handoff stalls.
No problem found

Handle the awkward update when the symptom cannot be duplicated yet

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] at [Shop Name]. We completed the initial diagnostic check on your [Vehicle] and we have not duplicated the original symptom yet.

That does not always mean nothing is wrong, but it does mean the next best step may be [additional testing / monitoring / scheduling when the symptom is active]. Call or text us back at [Phone Number] and we will walk you through the options.
  • Useful for intermittent issues that can easily turn into frustrated back-and-forth.
  • Prevents the customer from hearing “we found nothing” without context or next steps.
Parts + price question

Reassure the customer when the diagnosis is done but the customer is hesitating on cost

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. We have the issue identified on your [Vehicle], and I wanted to make sure you had the repair recommendation and pricing in front of you.

If the main question is total cost, timing, or whether the repair can be split into phases, call or text us back at [Phone Number]. We can talk through the best next step so the car does not stay stuck in limbo.
  • Best for soft objections after the diagnostic findings are already known.
  • Opens the door for phased work, financing, or priority sequencing without immediately discounting.
Ready for repair

Confirm the shop can move immediately once the customer approves

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] at [Shop Name]. We are ready to move your [Vehicle] from diagnosis into repair as soon as we have your approval.

If you want us to get started, call or text us back at [Phone Number]. Once we hear from you, we can lock in the next timing update and keep the job moving.
  • Useful when the shop has already done the hard part and just needs the go-ahead.
  • Helps convert “I need to think about it” drift into a concrete decision point.
