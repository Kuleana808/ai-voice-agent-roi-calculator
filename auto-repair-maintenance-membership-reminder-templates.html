Auto repair maintenance membership reminder script library

Use these as practical starting points, not compliance or customer-service policy advice. The job is simple: re-open the membership or prepaid-service conversation, reduce lapsed renewals, and give customers a clear next step when service-plan reminders, inspection bundles, and stale recurring-service follow-up would otherwise die in the front-desk pile.

Plan renewal

Reminder when a maintenance membership or prepaid plan is ready to renew

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I wanted to follow up because your [maintenance membership / prepaid oil change plan / service bundle] for your [Vehicle] is ready to renew.

If you want to keep your plan active, call or text us back at [Phone Number]. We can help you lock in the next visit and keep the included service from slipping through the cracks.
  • Best for oil-change clubs, prepaid maintenance bundles, fleet-lite service plans, and any recurring-service offer that needs a renewal touch before it quietly lapses.
  • Creates urgency without sounding like a generic discount blast.
Prepaid package balance

Reminder when a customer still has unused visits or value inside a prepaid service package

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] at [Shop Name]. We’re reaching out because your [service plan / prepaid maintenance package] still has [visit / inspection / tire rotation / oil change] value available for your [Vehicle].

If you want to use the next included service, call or text us back at [Phone Number] and we’ll help you book it before the plan sits unused.
  • Useful for prepaid oil-change bundles, inspection plans, tire-rotation packages, and recurring maintenance offers that customers forget to use.
  • Works well when the shop tracks remaining visits, renewal dates, or unused service credits inside the CRM or shop-management system.
Inspection bundle

Reminder when a bundled inspection or seasonal check is included in the customer’s plan

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I wanted to remind you that your [inspection / seasonal check / included maintenance visit] is still available as part of your [membership / prepaid plan] for your [Vehicle].

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] if you want to get it on the schedule now and avoid letting that included service go unused.
  • Best for annual inspection bundles, seasonal road-trip checks, battery/AC checkups, and plan-based service visits that are already part of the offer.
  • Gives customers a clear value-driven reason to respond now.
Lapsed membership

Re-open the conversation when a service plan or recurring package quietly lapsed

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] with [Shop Name]. I’m following up on your [maintenance membership / prepaid service package] for your [Vehicle] because I don’t want that plan to quietly lapse if you still want the included service.

If you want to reactivate it, call or text us back at [Phone Number]. We can help you pick the next visit and get the plan back in motion.
  • Useful when the membership renewed once or twice, then went quiet after the customer got busy, changed timing, or forgot what was included.
  • Bridges naturally into prepaid-plan recovery without sounding like a hard close.
Seasonal renewal push

Reminder tied to seasonal plan demand like summer travel, winter weather, or inspection season

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I wanted to check in before [summer travel / winter weather / inspection season] picks up because your [maintenance plan / seasonal service package] for your [Vehicle] is a good fit for the next [battery / AC / cooling / tire / brake] visit.

If you want to get ahead of it now, call or text us back at [Phone Number] and we’ll help you schedule the best next step inside the plan.
  • Best for seasonal inspection bundles, AC tune-up plans, battery checks, tire service bundles, and road-trip-prep memberships.
  • Makes the renewal feel timely instead of random.
Non-renewal follow-up

One more touch when earlier renewal reminders got ignored

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I wanted to make one more follow-up attempt on the maintenance membership or prepaid-service reminder for your [Vehicle].

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] if you want help deciding whether to renew, use the next included visit, or update the plan for what your vehicle needs now.
  • Useful when the customer did not respond to the first renewal touch but is still worth reactivating.
  • Keeps the tone helpful instead of sounding like a hard retention chase.
Advisor membership follow-through

Reminder when the team promised a plan follow-up and the customer is waiting

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] at [Shop Name]. I wanted to follow through on the maintenance membership reminder we mentioned for your [Vehicle] so you have a clear next step.

If you want to renew or use the next included service, call or text us back at [Phone Number] and we’ll help you find the fastest opening that works for you.
  • Useful when an advisor promised to circle back after checking renewal timing, plan options, or schedule openings.
  • Reduces trust leaks caused by inconsistent manual membership follow-up.
Voicemail + renewal text combo

Short voicemail to pair with a membership-renewal SMS follow-up

Hi, this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I’m calling with a quick reminder about your maintenance membership or prepaid-service plan for your vehicle.

Please check the text message we just sent or call us back at [Phone Number] so we can help you renew or schedule the next included visit. Thank you.
  • Good when the shop wants one standard voicemail that matches its renewal-text process.
  • Keeps the message brief while moving the customer into an easier renewal reply channel.
