Auto repair service reminder script library

Use these as practical starting points, not compliance or customer-service policy advice. The job is simple: re-open the service conversation, reduce lapsed maintenance follow-up, and give customers a clear next step when overdue intervals, missed reminders, and stale repair-order follow-up would otherwise die in the front-desk pile.

Overdue maintenance

Reminder when a recommended service interval has already passed

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I wanted to follow up because your [oil change / factory maintenance / mileage-based service] is now overdue for your [Vehicle].

If you want to get it scheduled, call or text us back at [Phone Number]. We can help you lock in the next opening before the service delay turns into a bigger issue.
  • Best for oil changes, factory-scheduled maintenance, and mileage-based follow-up that slipped past the original due date.
  • Creates urgency without sounding like a generic coupon blast.
Mileage milestone

Reminder when the vehicle is due for service based on usage or time

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] at [Shop Name]. We’re reaching out because your [Vehicle] should be getting close to its next recommended service interval.

If you want to get ahead of it now, call or text us back at [Phone Number] and we’ll help you book the right maintenance before it becomes a bigger repair.
  • Useful for transmission service, brake inspections, tune-ups, fluid service, and manufacturer interval reminders.
  • Works well when the shop tracks mileage or last-service timing inside the CRM or shop-management system.
Inspection due

Reminder when a state inspection, safety check, or follow-up inspection is coming up

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I wanted to remind you that your [inspection / safety check / registration-related service] is coming up for your [Vehicle].

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] if you want to get it on the schedule now and avoid the last-minute rush.
  • Best for annual inspections, emissions-related timing, safety checks, and pre-registration reminders.
  • Gives customers a clear calendar-driven reason to respond now.
Lapsed repair order

Re-open the conversation when a customer approved nothing and then disappeared

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] with [Shop Name]. I’m following up on the service recommendations we discussed for your [Vehicle] because I don’t want that file to go stale if you still need help.

If you want to move forward, call or text us back at [Phone Number]. We can help you prioritize the next step and get the work scheduled.
  • Useful when the repair order went quiet after inspection, estimate review, or a “let me think about it” conversation.
  • Bridges naturally into declined-work recovery without sounding like a hard close.
Seasonal service

Reminder tied to summer travel, winter weather, or other seasonal stress

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I wanted to check in before [summer travel / winter weather / storm season] picks up because your [Vehicle] may be due for [battery / AC / cooling / tire / brake] service.

If you want to get ahead of it now, call or text us back at [Phone Number] and we’ll help you schedule the best next step.
  • Best for AC service, cooling checks, battery health, tires, and road-trip prep.
  • Makes the reminder feel timely instead of random.
No-response reminder

One more touch when earlier reminder attempts got ignored

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I wanted to make one more follow-up attempt on the maintenance or service reminder for your [Vehicle].

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] if you want help deciding what should happen next or which service should be handled first.
  • Useful when the customer did not respond to the first reminder but is still worth reactivating.
  • Keeps the tone helpful instead of sounding like a collections-style chase.
Advisor follow-through

Reminder when the team promised a follow-up and the customer is waiting

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] at [Shop Name]. I wanted to follow through on the service reminder we mentioned for your [Vehicle] so you have a clear next step.

If you want to get it scheduled, call or text us back at [Phone Number] and we’ll help you find the fastest opening that works for you.
  • Useful when an advisor promised to circle back after checking parts, schedule openings, or recommended timing.
  • Reduces trust leaks caused by inconsistent manual follow-up.
Voicemail + text combo

Short voicemail to pair with a service-reminder SMS follow-up

Hi, this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I’m calling with a quick reminder about service coming up for your vehicle.

Please check the text message we just sent or call us back at [Phone Number] so we can help you schedule the next step. Thank you.
  • Good when the shop wants one standard voicemail that matches its reminder text process.
  • Keeps the message brief while moving the customer into an easier reply channel.
