Auto repair voicemail follow-up script library

Use these as practical starting points, not compliance or customer-service policy advice. The job is simple: reduce phone tag, keep work moving, and give customers a clear next step when service updates, approvals, and pickup coordination would otherwise sit in a voicemail pile.

Repair-status update

Quick voicemail when the customer wants a status check

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I’m calling with a quick update on your [Vehicle / Repair Order].

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] when you have a moment and we’ll walk you through the current repair status, what’s been completed, and the next step from here.
  • Good for standard repair-progress callbacks that would otherwise trigger repeat inbound calls.
  • Keeps the message short enough to be returned quickly.
Inspection reminder

Voicemail for pending diagnostic approval or recommended work

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] at [Shop Name]. We finished the inspection on your [Vehicle] and wanted to review the findings with you before we move forward.

Please give us a call back at [Phone Number] so we can go over the recommended work, timing, and anything you want approved today.
  • Best when inspection findings are ready but the approval conversation is stuck in voicemail.
  • Creates urgency without sounding pushy.
Estimate follow-up

Voicemail when a service estimate has gone quiet

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I’m following up on the estimate we prepared for your [Vehicle / Service Request].

If you still want to move ahead, call or text us back at [Phone Number]. If you have questions about timing, parts, or price, we can walk through those with you so the estimate doesn’t go cold.
  • Useful after a customer requests pricing but never confirms the work.
  • Helps surface hesitation before the job disappears.
Parts delay

Voicemail when the repair is waiting on parts or supplier timing

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] with [Shop Name]. Quick update on your [Vehicle]: we are still waiting on [part / supplier confirmation / delivery timing] before we can finish the repair timeline.

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] if you want the latest ETA or want to talk through the next step while we wait.
  • Reduces uncertainty when customers assume silence means the shop forgot them.
  • Works well when paired with a same-day text follow-up.
Pickup coordination

Voicemail when the vehicle is ready or nearly ready

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I’m calling because your [Vehicle] is ready for pickup / almost ready and we want to lock in the best pickup time with you.

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] so we can confirm timing, payment questions, and any final details before pickup.
  • Useful when finished work sits because the final handoff never gets scheduled cleanly.
  • Keeps the closeout process from turning into another round of voicemail tag.
After-hours callback

Voicemail for leads that reached out after the shop was closed

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. We saw your call about your vehicle and wanted to get back to you as soon as we could.

Call or text us at [Phone Number] and we’ll help with the next step, whether that’s scheduling service, reviewing an estimate, or checking repair timing.
  • Best for no-start calls, towing follow-up, or urgent repair questions that came in after hours.
  • Gives the customer one clear reply path instead of another dead end.
Advisor catch-up

Voicemail when multiple updates stacked up and the customer has gone cold

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] with [Shop Name]. I wanted to make one more follow-up attempt on your [Vehicle / Repair Order] so we can get everything moving again.

Please call or text us back at [Phone Number] and we’ll help you with the fastest next step, whether that’s approval, scheduling, parts timing, or pickup coordination.
  • Useful when the file is active but the conversation keeps breaking.
  • Creates a simple reset message without rewriting the whole history.
Voicemail + text combo

Short voicemail to pair with an SMS follow-up

Hi, this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I’m calling with a quick update on your vehicle / estimate.

Please check the text message we just sent or call us back at [Phone Number] so we can help with the next step. Thank you.
  • Good when the shop wants one standard voicemail that matches its callback text process.
  • Keeps the message brief while moving the customer into an easier reply channel.
