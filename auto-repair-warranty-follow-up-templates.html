Auto repair warranty follow-up script library

Use these as practical starting points, not compliance or customer-service policy advice. The job is simple: explain what is covered, reassure the customer that you are still accountable after the repair, reduce preventable comebacks, and give customers a clear next step when warranty questions or expiring workmanship guarantees would otherwise sit unanswered.

Coverage explanation

Follow-up after pickup to explain what the repair warranty actually covers

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I wanted to follow up on the [Repair] we completed on your [Vehicle].

Your repair is covered through [date / mileage / terms], and if anything connected to that work feels off, call or text us right away so we can help before it turns into a bigger headache.

If you want, I can also resend the warranty details and the fastest number to call if a question comes up.
Comeback prevention

Check-in a few days later to catch issues before the customer feels ignored

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. Just checking in after your recent [Repair / Service] on the [Vehicle].

How is everything feeling so far? If you have any concern about noise, warning lights, drivability, or how the repair is performing, reply here or call us and we will help you quickly while the details are still fresh.
Workmanship reminder

Reminder before a workmanship guarantee or recheck window expires

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. Quick heads-up that the workmanship follow-up window for your recent [Repair] ends on [Date].

If anything has felt off, or if you want us to give the vehicle a quick recheck before that date, call or text us back and we will get you on the schedule.
Parts + labor clarity

When a customer is confused about what the warranty covers

Hi [First Name], happy to clarify the warranty on your recent repair.

The coverage applies to [parts / labor / both] related to the work completed on [Date]. If the same issue comes back or the repaired area is acting up, contact us first so we can document it and walk you through the next step under the warranty terms.
Recheck scheduling

Prompt to book the quick follow-up inspection many customers forget

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. We still have your post-repair recheck open for the [Repair / System] we worked on.

If you want to lock in a quick follow-up inspection, call or text us back and we will reserve a time. It is the easiest way to confirm everything is settling in the way it should.
Complaint recovery

De-escalation follow-up when a customer sounds frustrated after the repair

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. I saw your note about the concern after your recent [Repair], and I wanted to reach out directly.

We take post-repair issues seriously. If you call or text us back with the best time to reconnect, we will review what is happening and get the right next step in motion fast.
Voicemail script

Short voicemail for warranty follow-up and post-repair reassurance

Hi [First Name], this is [Name] from [Shop Name]. Just following up on the recent work we completed on your [Vehicle].

If you have any questions about the warranty, the repair, or want a quick recheck before your follow-up window expires, call us back at [Phone Number]. Again, [Name] from [Shop Name], and our number is [Phone Number].
