250-400 word listing copy

The AI Voice Agent ROI Calculator helps local service businesses estimate how much revenue they may be losing from missed calls and whether an AI phone agent could pay for itself.

Users enter six simple assumptions: missed calls per month, booking rate, average revenue per booked job, gross margin, monthly software cost, and setup cost. The calculator returns estimated missed revenue, monthly breakeven recovered calls, base-case monthly net gain, setup payback timing, and conservative/base/aggressive recovery scenarios.

This tool is built for call-driven operators such as plumbers, HVAC companies, electricians, dentists, med spas, veterinary clinics, garage door companies, roofers, pest control businesses, urgent care clinics, physical therapy clinics, chiropractic clinics, orthopedic clinics, ophthalmology clinics, dermatology clinics, plastic surgery practices, and similar local businesses.

Instead of reading generic AI-sales copy, owners can self-qualify with their own numbers, then use the prefilled email CTA to request a rollout plan.

Best fit categories: AI tools, sales ops, lead generation, local SEO, customer support automation, voice AI, and SMB operations.
