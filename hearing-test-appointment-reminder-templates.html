Hearing test reminder template library

Use these as practical starting points, not polished marketing copy. The job is simple: reduce no-shows, make next steps clear, prep patients for arrival, and keep hearing-test or hearing-aid consult demand from slipping through the cracks when the front desk is overloaded.

Confirmation SMS

New hearing-test appointment confirmation

Hi [First Name] — this is [Practice Name]. Your hearing test is scheduled for [Day, Date] at [Time].

Please reply YES to confirm or call/text us here if you need to reschedule. We look forward to seeing you.
  • Use right after booking to lock in commitment.
  • Best when the practice wants a text confirmation trail.
24-hour reminder

Standard hearing-test reminder text

Reminder from [Practice Name]: your hearing test is tomorrow at [Time].

If you have hearing aids, a referral, insurance card, or recent hearing records, please bring them with you. Reply here if you have any questions.
  • Reduces day-before uncertainty and incomplete arrivals.
  • Add or remove prep items based on your intake workflow.
Same-day reminder

Morning-of appointment reminder

Hi [First Name] — just a quick reminder that your appointment with [Practice Name] is today at [Time].

If anything changes, please call or text us as soon as you can so we can help with next steps.
  • Useful for reducing same-day no-shows.
  • Pairs well with a voicemail fallback when messages go unanswered.
Hearing-aid consult

Hearing aid evaluation reminder

Hi [First Name] — this is [Practice Name]. Just confirming your hearing aid consult on [Day] at [Time].

If another person helps you make hearing decisions, feel free to bring them with you. Reply here if you need to adjust the appointment.
  • Designed for higher-consideration consults where attendance matters.
  • Encourages accompaniment without sounding pushy.
Insurance / paperwork

Insurance-benefit and intake reminder

Reminder from [Practice Name]: before your visit on [Day], please bring your photo ID, insurance card, and any referral or prior hearing-test paperwork you have available.

If you are unsure what to bring, reply here and we will help.
  • Best for reducing front-desk friction and incomplete check-ins.
  • Can be sent 24-48 hours before the visit.
Missed confirmation follow-up

No-reply reminder text

Hi [First Name] — we have not heard back yet about your appointment with [Practice Name] on [Day] at [Time].

Please reply YES to confirm or let us know if you need to reschedule so we can hold the right time for you.
  • Useful when patients have not confirmed after the first reminder.
  • Creates a clean prompt without sounding like a threat.
Voicemail fallback

Hearing-test reminder voicemail script

Hi, this is [Name] with [Practice Name]. I am calling to remind you about your hearing-test appointment on [Day] at [Time].

If you need to confirm, reschedule, or ask a question before you come in, please call or text us back at this number. Thank you.
  • Useful when text opt-in is unavailable or the patient prefers calls.
  • Keeps the script short enough for staff consistency.
Reschedule / save the booking

Gentle reschedule message

Hi [First Name] — no problem if [Day/Time] no longer works for your hearing-test appointment.

Reply with a better day or time and we will help you reschedule as quickly as possible.
  • Good for keeping the conversation alive instead of losing the patient entirely.
  • Works well after a cancellation or missed-call follow-up.
