Missed-call recovery template library

Start with the universal scripts, then use the niche variants when the buyer journey changes. These are meant to be practical and sendable, not clever. The job is to acknowledge the missed call, reduce drop-off, and move the prospect toward a reply, booking, or callback.

Universal SMS

Simple missed-call text back

Hi [First Name] — sorry we missed your call. This is [Business Name]. How can we help?

If it's easier, text us a quick summary here and we'll reply as soon as possible.
  • Best during business hours when the team can actually respond.
  • Use when you want the caller to text the need instead of bouncing.
After hours

After-hours text back

Hi [First Name] — thanks for calling [Business Name]. We missed your call, but we did get your message.

If this is urgent, reply here with a short note and the best callback number. If not, we will reach back out when we reopen at [Opening Time].
  • Useful for nights, weekends, lunch gaps, and overflow.
  • Sets expectations without leaving the lead in silence.
Voicemail fallback

Short missed-call voicemail script

Hi, this is [Name] with [Business Name]. Sorry we missed your call.

Please call or text us back at this number with your name and what you need help with, and we'll get back to you as soon as we can. Thank you.
  • Good for teams that want one standard callback voicemail.
  • Pairs well with an SMS sent right after the voicemail drop.
