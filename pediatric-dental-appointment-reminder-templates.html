Pediatric dental reminder template library

Use these as practical starting points, not polished marketing copy. The job is simple: reduce no-shows, make prep steps obvious for parents, save same-day gaps when kids get sick or schedules change, and keep new-patient or urgent dental demand from slipping through the cracks when the front desk is overloaded.

Confirmation SMS

New-patient appointment confirmation

Hi [Parent First Name] — this is [Practice Name]. [Child First Name]'s appointment is scheduled for [Day, Date] at [Time].

Please reply YES to confirm or call/text us here if you need to reschedule. We look forward to seeing you both.
  • Use right after booking to lock in commitment.
  • Best when the practice wants a text confirmation trail tied to the parent or guardian.
24-hour reminder

Standard pediatric dental reminder text

Reminder from [Practice Name]: [Child First Name]'s appointment is tomorrow at [Time].

Please arrive a few minutes early and bring your insurance card, any referral paperwork, and completed forms if you have them. Reply here if you have any questions.
  • Reduces day-before uncertainty and incomplete arrivals.
  • Easy default for cleanings, exams, fillings, and consult visits.
Same-day reminder

Morning-of appointment reminder

Hi [Parent First Name] — just a quick reminder that [Child First Name] has an appointment with [Practice Name] today at [Time].

If anything changes, please call or text us as soon as you can so we can help with next steps.
  • Useful for reducing same-day no-shows.
  • Pairs well with a voicemail fallback when texts go unanswered.
Sedation / prep

Nitrous or sedation prep reminder

Hi [Parent First Name] — this is [Practice Name] with a reminder for [Child First Name]'s visit on [Day] at [Time].

Please review the prep instructions we sent and reply here if you have any questions before the appointment. If fasting, arrival, or medication instructions are unclear, let us know so we can help before the visit.
  • Designed for treatment visits where prep mistakes create last-minute cancellations.
  • Keeps the tone calm while protecting higher-value chair time.
Insurance / paperwork

Insurance and forms reminder

Reminder from [Practice Name]: before [Child First Name]'s visit on [Day], please bring your photo ID, insurance card, and any referral or completed forms you have available.

If you are unsure what to bring, reply here and we will help.
  • Best for reducing front-desk friction and incomplete check-ins.
  • Can be sent 24-48 hours before the visit.
Missed confirmation follow-up

No-reply reminder text

Hi [Parent First Name] — we have not heard back yet about [Child First Name]'s appointment with [Practice Name] on [Day] at [Time].

Please reply YES to confirm or let us know if you need to reschedule so we can hold the right time for you.
  • Useful when parents have not confirmed after the first reminder.
  • Creates a clean prompt without sounding harsh or guilt-heavy.
Voicemail fallback

Pediatric dental reminder voicemail script

Hi, this is [Name] with [Practice Name]. I am calling to remind you about [Child First Name]'s appointment on [Day] at [Time].

If you need to confirm, reschedule, or ask a question before you come in, please call or text us back at this number. Thank you.
  • Useful when text opt-in is unavailable or the parent prefers calls.
  • Keeps the script short enough for staff consistency.
Reschedule / save the booking

Gentle recall or cancellation reschedule message

Hi [Parent First Name] — no problem if [Day/Time] no longer works for [Child First Name]'s appointment.

Reply with a better day or time and we will help you reschedule as quickly as possible.
  • Good for keeping the conversation alive instead of losing the family entirely.
  • Works well after a cancellation, school conflict, or missed-call follow-up.
